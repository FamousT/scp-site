Our practices

We deploy creativity and innovation to assist clients in resolving intricate issues. Acting as a proactive business partner, we help to identify potential business risks, offering practical advice to manage and mitigate these risks effectively.

We focus on key areas including competition, private equity, commercial litigation, project finance, alternative energy, ESG, taxation, public finance, regulatory and compliance, and family & wealth management and estate practice. Collaborating with in-house counsel, we assist in designing robust compliance programs across a range of sectors such as financial services, FMCG, energy, retail, and technology, among others.
